# Industry Profile: B2B SaaS

## Overview

B2B SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) is a business model where software is licensed on a subscription basis and centrally hosted. This profile covers the B2B SaaS industry as the initial vertical for Compass.

---

## Market Characteristics

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Market Size | $200B+ (2025), growing at 15-20% CAGR |
| Business Model | Subscription (monthly/annually), usage-based, hybrid |
| Customer Types | SMB (1-100 employees), Mid-Market (100-1000), Enterprise (1000+) |
| Sales Model | Self-serve, inside sales, field sales (by ACV tier) |
| Development Model | Agile/Scrum, continuous deployment, product-led |
| Typical Margins | 70-85% gross margin, 10-25% operating margin (profitable) |

## AI Opportunity Density by Department

| Department | Opportunity Density | Reasoning |
|------------|-------------------|-----------|
| Sales | Very High | Repetitive manual processes, high documentation burden, clear ROI |
| Support | Very High | High volume, repetitive patterns, knowledge management gap |
| CS | High | Data-rich but analysis-poor, proactive intervention opportunity |
| Marketing | High | Content production, attribution, personalization |
| Finance | Medium-High | AP/AR processes, reconciliation, reporting |
| Engineering | Medium | Code generation, documentation, testing |
| Product | Medium | Feedback synthesis, spec writing, user research |
| HR | Medium | Resume screening, scheduling, sentiment analysis |
| Legal | Medium | Contract review, compliance monitoring, clause management |
| Operations | Low-Medium | Provisioning, reporting, vendor management |
